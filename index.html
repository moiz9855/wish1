Happy Birthday Urwa

Umeed hai ke aaj ka din tumhare liye khushi aur sukoon ka din ho. Main nahi jaanta ke tum asal zindagi mein kaisi ho — kyunke hum kabhi mile nahi, sirf baatein hi hui hain. Lekin jitna tumhari baaton se samjha hai, tum ek seedhi, real aur thoughtful larki ho. Tumhari baaton mein ek simplicity hai jo rare hoti hai. Na dikhawa, na zyada lafz — bas ek asliyat jo achi lagti hai. Main dua karta hoon ke tumhara agla saal achi cheezon se bhara ho — tum woh logon se milo jo tumhari kadar karte hon, aur tumhare sapne dheere dheere haqeeqat ban jayein. Zindagi mushkil bhi hoti hai, lekin umeed karta hoon ke tum hamesha strong raho, aur khud ko kabhi chhota na samjho.
